Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CSGOAI

What is CSGOAI?

CSGOAI is a revolutionary gaming token that combines AI-powered analytics with GameFi rewards on the Solana blockchain. It enhances the CS:GO gaming experience by providing real-time strategic insights while allowing players to earn tokens through gameplay.

How does the AI analytics work?

Our AI system analyzes thousands of gameplay data points in real-time to provide strategic recommendations, heatmaps, and counter-strategies. It learns from professional matches and adapts to your playing style to offer personalized tips that improve your gameplay.

How can I earn CSGOAI tokens?

You can earn CSGOAI tokens through various activities: completing daily quests, achieving ranked milestones, participating in tournaments, staking your existing tokens, and contributing to the community through content creation and feedback.

Is CSGOAI secure?

Yes, CSGOAI is built on the Solana blockchain, known for its speed and security. Our smart contracts have been thoroughly audited by reputable third-party security firms. We also implement multi-signature wallets and secure escrow systems for all transactions.

Where can I buy CSGOAI tokens?

CSGOAI tokens are currently available on decentralized exchanges like Raydium, Orca, and Jupiter. We're also working on listings on centralized exchanges including MEXC, LBank, Gate.io, and Coinstore, which will be available in Q2-Q3 2025.
